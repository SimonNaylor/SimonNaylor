Why is Dogecoin Worthless?

Dogecoin, unlike Bitcoin, has no cap in terms of coins that can be minted. Bitcoin is capped at 21 million coins, whereas Dogecoin is a fiat currency. This currency rivals that of Zimbabwe, where the solution for Mugabe was to print more money, even if it was bad. There is no reason that it should go up in value, unless there is a strict cap. The United States has no currency cap, but we do have the FED, and many other regulatory bodies. This is something Dogecoin does not have and it seems like it never will.
